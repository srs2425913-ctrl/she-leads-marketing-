# SHE LEADS MARKETING

## Marketing Strategy Checklist

*Your 7-Step Guide to Building a Results-Driven Marketing Plan*

---

### How to Use This Checklist

This checklist walks you through the 7 essential pillars of a complete marketing strategy. For each item, check off what you already have in place — and identify the gaps holding your business back.

At the end, you'll have a clear picture of where to focus next.

---

### Step 1: Brand Foundation

- [ ] **Mission & Vision defined** — Do we know exactly *why* we exist and *where* we're going?
- [ ] **Target audience documented** — Have we defined our ideal client's demographics, pain points, and goals?
- [ ] **Brand voice guidelines** — Is our tone of voice consistent across all channels?
- [ ] **Visual identity established** — Do we have a logo, color palette, and typography system?
- [ ] **Tagline or key message** — Can we articulate our value proposition in one sentence?
- [ ] **Brand story developed** — Do we have a compelling narrative that connects with our audience?

> **Gap analysis:** *If you checked fewer than 4, start here. Without a strong foundation, every channel underperforms.*

---

### Step 2: Content Strategy

- [ ] **Content pillars identified** — Do we have 3–5 core topics that align with our expertise and audience needs?
- [ ] **Content calendar maintained** — Is there a schedule for blogs, social posts, emails, and assets?
- [ ] **Blog publishing consistent** — Are we publishing SEO-optimized content at least 1–2x per month?
- [ ] **Content repurposed** — Do we turn one piece of content into multiple formats (blog → social → email → video)?
- [ ] **High-value assets planned** — Do we create lead magnets (checklists, guides, templates) to capture emails?
- [ ] **Competitor content reviewed** — Do we know what our competitors are creating and how we can do it better?

> **Gap analysis:** *If you checked fewer than 3, your content may lack strategy. Consistency beats volume.*

---

### Step 3: Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

- [ ] **Keyword research completed** — Do we know which terms our ideal clients are searching for?
- [ ] **On-page SEO optimized** — Are title tags, meta descriptions, headers, and alt text in place?
- [ ] **Technical SEO audited** — Is the site mobile-friendly, fast-loading, and free of broken links?
- [ ] **Local SEO set up (if applicable)** — Is our Google Business Profile claimed and optimized?
- [ ] **Backlink strategy active** — Are we earning quality links from reputable sources?
- [ ] **Analytics tracked** — Do we monitor organic traffic, keyword rankings, and click-through rates?

> **Gap analysis:** *If you checked fewer than 3, you're leaving traffic on the table. SEO compounds over time — the sooner you start, the more you earn.*

---

### Step 4: Social Media

- [ ] **Platforms selected intentionally** — Are we active on the channels where our audience actually spends time?
- [ ] **Posting schedule consistent** — Do we post at least 3–5x per week on our primary platform?
- [ ] **Engagement active** — Do we respond to comments, DMs, and mentions within 24 hours?
- [ ] **Visual content diverse** — Do we mix static posts, Stories, Reels/Shorts, and carousels?
- [ ] **Community building** — Do we actively participate in conversations, not just broadcast?
- [ ] **Performance reviewed** — Do we track reach, engagement rate, and click-throughs per platform?

> **Gap analysis:** *If you checked fewer than 3, your social presence may be passive. Social media is a conversation, not a billboard.*

---

### Step 5: Email Marketing

- [ ] **Email list growing** — Do we have a lead magnet or opt-in to capture new subscribers?
- [ ] **Segmentation in place** — Are subscribers grouped by interest, behavior, or stage?
- [ ] **Welcome sequence active** — Do new subscribers receive an automated onboarding series?
- [ ] **Regular newsletter sent** — Do we nurture our list with valuable content at least 1–2x per month?
- [ ] **Promotional emails balanced** — Are we providing value before asking for the sale (80/20 rule)?
- [ ] **Metrics tracked** — Do we monitor open rates, click rates, and unsubscribe rates?

> **Gap analysis:** *If you checked fewer than 3, your email list may be under-leveraged. Email has the highest ROI of any marketing channel.*

---

### Step 6: Paid Advertising

- [ ] **Goals defined** — Are we clear on whether we're optimizing for awareness, leads, or sales?
- [ ] **Audience targeting set** — Do we have well-defined custom audiences and lookalikes?
- [ ] **Ad creative tested** — Are we running A/B tests on headlines, visuals, and CTAs?
- [ ] **Budget managed effectively** — Do we know our cost per lead (CPL) and return on ad spend (ROAS)?
- [ ] **Retargeting active** — Are we re-engaging visitors who didn't convert on the first visit?
- [ ] **Landing pages aligned** — Do our ads lead to optimized pages designed for conversion?

> **Gap analysis:** *If you checked fewer than 3, you may be burning budget. Paid ads work best when strategy, creative, and landing pages are aligned.*

---

### Step 7: Analytics & Optimization

- [ ] **KPIs established** — Do we have clear, measurable goals for each channel?
- [ ] **Regular reporting cadence** — Do we review performance weekly, monthly, and quarterly?
- [ ] **Data-driven decisions** — Do we let metrics guide our strategy, not just intuition?
- [ ] **Conversion tracking set up** — Are we tracking form fills, purchases, calls, and other key actions?
- [ ] **ROI calculated** — Do we know how much revenue each channel generates versus its cost?
- [ ] **Iteration loop active** — Do we test, learn, and adjust based on what the data tells us?

> **Gap analysis:** *If you checked fewer than 3, you're flying blind. What gets measured gets improved.*
