Meta Description: How much should you pay for social media management? We compare the value and deliverables across our five service tiers to help you find the right fit for your brand.

In 2026, social media management is no longer just about "posting photos." It is a complex ecosystem of community management, visual storytelling, SEO-driven captions, and data analysis. Because every business is at a different stage, a "one size fits all" pricing model no longer works.

At She Leads Marketing, we’ve structured our tiers to grow with you. Here is a breakdown of what social media management looks like at every price point and how to choose the right one for your Scalable Excellence.

The Starter Tier: $500/mo

The Goal: Professional Foundation. What You Get: 2 posts per week + 1 blog + 1 newsletter. Who it’s for: Solopreneurs and new founders who need to "look the part." At this level, we ensure you have a consistent, on-brand presence so that when people research you, they see a thriving, active business.

The Accelerate Tier: $1,500/mo

The Goal: Growth and Momentum. What You Get: 3 posts per week + 2 blogs + 1 newsletter + Ads + Email Flows. Who it’s for: Small teams ready to move beyond "foundation" and into "growth." This tier adds paid advertising and automated email flows to the mix, creating a more proactive lead-generation engine.

The Elevate Tier: $3,500/mo

The Goal: Market Dominance. What You Get: 5 posts per week + 4 blogs + 2 newsletters + 2 Ad Platforms. Who it’s for: Established businesses that want to own their niche. With daily-ish posting and multiple ad platforms (e.g., Instagram + Google), this tier is about volume and strategic positioning.

The Premium Tier: $6,000/mo

The Goal: High-Volume Leadership. What You Get: Daily social posts + 2 short-form videos/week + 8 blogs + Multi-platform Ads. Who it’s for: Aggressive brands that want to lead the conversation every single day. Short-form video (Reels, TikTok) is the most powerful growth lever in 2026, and this tier puts you at the front of that trend.

The Enterprise Tier: Custom

The Goal: Bespoke Strategic Alignment. Who it’s for: Large organizations with complex needs, requiring dedicated team support and fully custom deliverables.

Which Tier is Your Best ROI?

The best ROI doesn't always come from the cheapest package. It comes from the package that matches your business objectives:
  • Need to reclaim your time? Starter.
  • Need to scale lead volume? Accelerate.
  • Need to outperform competitors? Elevate.
  • Need to dominate your industry? Premium.

The She Leads Advantage: Unified Strategy

Regardless of the tier you choose, you benefit from our Unified Strategy. We don't just "manage" your social media; we integrate it into your entire brand ecosystem. Your social posts will always align with your SEO blogs, your email newsletters, and your core mission of women empowering women.
